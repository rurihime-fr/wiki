## Privacy

- [Proton Mail](https://proton.me/mail) - better privacy mail

- [TempMail](https://temp-mail.org/en/) - generate disposable emails

- [Zemail](https://zemail.me/), [Emailnator](https://www.emailnator.com/) - generate disposable gmails

- [Smail](https://smailpro.com/temporary-email) - generate disposable gmails and outlook

- [SimpleLogin](https://simplelogin.io/), [Addy](https://addy.io/) - send/receive mail without revealing your actual email (email aliasing)

## Extensions

:::info Never use multiple adblockers in a browser, they harm more than good
Just using ublock for adblocking should do your work.
:::

 **Must haves in both browsers**  
   - Brave - inbuilt adblocker, [Proton Pass](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/proton-pass-free-password/ghmbeldphafepmbegfdlkpapadhbakde?hl=en)

   - Firefox - [Ublock](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/ublock-origin/), [ProtonPass](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/proton-pass/)

- [Sponsor Block](https://sponsor.ajay.app/) - skip youtube sponsorships & shoutouts

- [Price Tracker](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/price-history-tracker-spe/ojplmecpdpgccookcobabopnaifgidhf) - track price history of ecommerce websites

- [ClearUrls](https://docs.clearurls.xyz/) - remove tracking elements from URLs

- [Return YouTube Dislike](https://returnyoutubedislike.com/) - unhides the dislike count on youtube
